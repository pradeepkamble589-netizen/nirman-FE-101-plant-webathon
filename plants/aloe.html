Aloe Vera 🌵

Scientific Name

Aloe vera

Family

Asphodelaceae

Description

Aloe vera is a succulent plant known for its medicinal and cosmetic properties. It stores water in its thick leaves, making it drought-resistant and ideal for hot climates.

Habitat

Aloe vera grows in tropical and subtropical regions. It prefers well-drained soil and full sunlight.

Benefits

  • Heals burns and wounds
  • Moisturizes skin
  • Boosts digestion and immunity
  • Anti-inflammatory and antioxidant properties

Uses

  • Gel used in skincare and haircare products
  • Consumed as juice for health benefits
  • Used in cosmetic creams and ointments

Environmental Importance

  • Helps prevent soil erosion
  • Provides oxygen and supports biodiversity
  • Suitable for xeriscaping and drought-prone areas
