Neem Tree 🌿

Scientific Name

Azadirachta indica

Family

Meliaceae

Description

The neem tree is a fast-growing evergreen tree native to the Indian subcontinent. It is widely known for its medicinal properties and is sometimes called the "Village Pharmacy". Neem can grow up to 15–20 meters tall.

Habitat

Neem trees grow best in tropical and semi-tropical climates. They thrive in dry and sandy soils but can tolerate poor soils. Commonly found in India, Southeast Asia, and Africa.

Benefits

  • Anti-bacterial, anti-fungal, and anti-inflammatory properties
  • Supports healthy skin and hair
  • Boosts immunity
  • Used in dental care (neem sticks)

Uses

  • Leaves used for medicinal purposes
  • Neem oil for skincare and haircare
  • Twigs used as natural toothbrushes
  • Extracts used in pesticides and insect repellents

Environmental Importance

  • Provides shade and oxygen
  • Helps maintain ecological balance
  • Used for soil enrichment and medicinal plantations
