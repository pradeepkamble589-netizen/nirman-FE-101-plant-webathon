Mango Tree 🌳

Scientific Name

Mangifera indica

Family

Anacardiaceae

Description

The mango tree is a tropical evergreen tree known as the "King of Fruits". It can grow up to 30–40 meters tall and produces sweet, juicy fruits. Mango trees are widely grown in tropical countries such as India.

Habitat

Mango trees grow best in warm climates with plenty of sunlight. They prefer well-drained soil and are commonly found in India and other tropical regions.

Benefits

  • Rich in Vitamin A and Vitamin C
  • Helps improve immunity
  • Supports healthy digestion
  • Good for skin and eyes

Uses

  • Mango fruits are eaten fresh
  • Used to make juices, smoothies, desserts, and pickles
  • Mango leaves are used in traditional ceremonies

Environmental Importance

  • Provides shade and oxygen
  • Helps maintain ecological balance
  • Provides shelter and food for birds and insects
