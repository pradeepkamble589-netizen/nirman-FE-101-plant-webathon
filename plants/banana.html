Banana Plant 🍌

Scientific Name

Musa spp.

Family

Musaceae

Description

Banana is a fast-growing herbaceous plant. Its fruit is widely consumed around the world and is rich in potassium and vitamins.

Habitat

Thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with plenty of water and sunlight.

Benefits

  • Rich in potassium and Vitamin C
  • Supports heart health and digestion
  • Boosts energy
  • Good for skin and hair health

Uses

  • Fruits eaten fresh or used in smoothies, desserts, and baking
  • Leaves used in packaging and traditional rituals
  • Fiber used in textiles

Environmental Importance

  • Provides shade and oxygen
  • Supports biodiversity by providing food for insects and animals
