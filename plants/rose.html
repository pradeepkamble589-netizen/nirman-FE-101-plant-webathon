Rose 🌹

Scientific Name

Rosa spp.

Family

Rosaceae

Description

Rose is a flowering plant known for its beautiful and fragrant blooms. It is cultivated worldwide for decoration, perfumes, and medicinal purposes.

Habitat

Thrives in well-drained soil and sunny locations. Roses prefer temperate climates.

Benefits

  • Fragrant flowers used in perfumes
  • Rose petals have medicinal properties
  • Supports mental wellbeing
  • Attracts pollinators like bees and butterflies

Uses

  • Ornamental plant in gardens and parks
  • Petals used in rose water and cosmetics
  • Medicinal teas from petals and hips

Environmental Importance

  • Supports pollinators
  • Provides oxygen
  • Improves aesthetic appeal of landscapes
