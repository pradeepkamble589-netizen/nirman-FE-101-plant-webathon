Peepal Tree 🌳

Scientific Name

Ficus religiosa

Family

Moraceae

Description

Peepal tree, also called Bodhi tree, is sacred in India. It is known for its large leaves and long lifespan, often living for hundreds of years.

Habitat

Grows in tropical and subtropical climates, often near temples or community areas.

Benefits

  • Provides oxygen even at night
  • Leaves used in traditional medicine
  • Supports spiritual and mental wellbeing

Uses

  • Used in religious and cultural rituals
  • Leaves and bark for medicinal purposes
  • Shade tree in public areas

Environmental Importance

  • Provides habitat for birds and insects
  • Maintains ecological balance
  • Reduces carbon dioxide in atmosphere
